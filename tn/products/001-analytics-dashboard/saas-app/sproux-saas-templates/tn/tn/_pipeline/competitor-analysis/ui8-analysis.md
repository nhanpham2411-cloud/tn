# SaaS Dashboard Template Market Analysis

**Date**: 2026-02-28
**Scope**: UI8, Creative Market, Figma Community, Gumroad, ThemeForest

---

## Market Overview

SaaS dashboard templates are one of the **top 3 best-selling categories** across all design marketplaces. The market is growing, driven by:
- Indie hackers and startups needing quick MVP dashboards
- Designers wanting pre-built starting points
- Demand for dark mode and Figma Variables support
- Shift from Sketch to Figma as primary design tool

### Underserved Niches (High Opportunity)

1. **HR / People Ops** — Score 13 but Competition=1 (very few quality products). SprouX has all needed components (Table, Avatar, Badge, Form, Tabs). Low competition + growing demand from remote-first companies.

2. **Healthcare Dashboard** — Score 13, Competition=2. MedKit (180 screens) and Clinexa (118 screens) exist but few with proper design system architecture. Regulatory-focused data display is unique.

3. **Analytics Dashboard** — Score 17, highest total. Competition=3 (moderate) but our differentiator (1041+ variables) is unique. Best first product due to broad appeal.
